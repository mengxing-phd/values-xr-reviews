Consideration of Human Values in Extended Reality: A Systematic Review

This webpage provides the supplementary material for the paper “Consideration of Human Values in Extended Reality: A Systematic Review”, submitted to IEEE VR 2026. It presents additional details behind the research, including a Summary Table and an associated Sankey Diagram.

The Summary Table includes key information for all 41 reviewed studies, such as Paper ID, title, citation, year, publication type, source, research motivation, technology types, XR application domains, methods, human values, future directions, and download links for each paper's PDF.

The Sankey Diagram provides a visual overview of how themes and sub-themes are distributed across the reviewed literature. It highlights patterns across categories, including motivations, technologies, application domains, methods, target groups, human values, and future directions in existing XR research.
